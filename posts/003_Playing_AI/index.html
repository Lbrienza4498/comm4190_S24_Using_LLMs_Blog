Discovering My Real Housewives Persona: A Journey Through Personal Identity and Image

In the world of marketing, understanding one’s personal brand is just as important as any product and their brand. For a recent assignment in my marketing class, I embarked on a journey to compare my personal identity with the image perceived by others. The project required me to gather feedback from friends, family, and peers, asking them to describe me in five words and to identify positive attributes they believe I lack.

The Assignment: A Reflection of Self

The responses from the 14 participants, a blend of those closest to me, painted an overwhelmingly positive picture of my personal image. People see me as sociable, outgoing, humorous, caring, and wise. Descriptors like “outgoing,” “talkative,” and “personable” suggest I am at ease in social situations and adaptable to various people and contexts. With frequent mentions of “funny,” “witty,” “comical,” and “sassy,” it’s evident that my sense of humor is a highlight among my social circles.

Beyond humor, I am recognized as a supportive friend and family member, with terms such as “listener,” “thoughtful,” “caring,” and “empathetic” used to characterize me. Being referred to as “wise” signals that others see me as a source of advice, while “genuine,” “loyal,” “bubbly,” “amazing,” and “joyful” confirm that my positive energy and good character do not go unnoticed.

Uncovering the Not-So-Obvious

Interestingly, the exercise also revealed what I am not. Words like “patient,” “relaxed,” and “unbothered” indicate that I may come across as a dynamic individual who reacts sometimes too quickly to my surroundings, also sometimes hastily or with a hot temper. The feedback suggests that I am not always the calmest or most punctual person, which aligns with my self-admitted poor texting habits.

The Real Housewives Tagline

As a big reality TV fan, fantasizing about being on a show like “The Real Housewives” is a daily activity for me. With the insights from my assignment, I prompted an AI tool, ChatGPT 4 preview, to create a Real Housewives tagline for me. This is what I prompted the AI tool with: “Here is some information about me from an assignment that assesses my personal brand, image, and identity:”Lucas Brienza Personal Brand Analysis: Identity vs Image….” With this knowledge about me in mind, please come up with a Real Housewives tagline for me as if I were on one of the franchises. Make sure to take into account different taglines from different housewife franchises in order to be accurate in your writing style and be creative !” The result was spot-on:

“In the game of life, I’m not just playing—I’m playing to win.” “Loyal to my roots and fiery to the core, when I step into the room, I bring the heat without missing a beat!”

Which Housewife Am I Most Alike?

Curiosity led me to ask the AI which Real Housewife I resemble the most, and the answer was Teresa Giudice from “The Real Housewives of New Jersey.” I asked the AI tool what housewife, any city past or present, I am most alike and why: “Based on the information you’ve provided, your personality seems to resonate closely with the energy and characteristics of Teresa Giudice from”The Real Housewives of New Jersey.” The comparison was drawn based on our shared Italian American heritage, fiery personalities, warmth, humor, struggles with vulnerability, and occasional unresponsiveness.

The Least Like Me: Lisa Vanderpump

On the other hand, I asked the opposite (who I am the least like and why) and the results were surprising: “Based on the traits you have described about yourself, one Real Housewife you may be least like is Lisa Vanderpump from”The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.” They cited her calm and poised demeanor, emotional reservation, strategic approach to relationships, British formality, and a lifestyle focused on business and philanthropy.

Embracing My Identity

The AI’s focus on my Italian American identity in these comparisons is intriguing. It highlights the cultural aspects of my persona, contrasting them with the British reserve of Lisa Vanderpump, while underscoring the similarities with Teresa Giudice’s vivacious Italian energy.

Conclusion

This assignment and the playful exploration with AI have not only provided a deeper understanding of how others perceive me but also allowed me to see myself through the lens of a reality TV star. With these insights, maybe ill break out as a future Reality TV star
